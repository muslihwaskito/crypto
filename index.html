The crypto crime club.

Crypto Crime Club is a Convict metaverse Game that lives in the near future. In the game metaverse 10 randomly generated Convicts live on the Binance blockchain, and are ready for action. They have endured true fortitude, to scrape a few "coins" together. Playing the metaverse game because the future looks cruel, but crypto looks bright. This is their last frontier. Join us as we embark on a journey to fat wallets, and wild times.

Each convict is truly unique and created with an open world metaverse-based future in mind. Such things are still being developed, and will be cultivated and grown by the community. We will focus on charitable contributions, helping righteous social causes, and embracing the past criminals of us all. The best part? This is all for the right purpose.
